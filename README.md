digital-literacy-project
This Digital Literacy Project was created as part of the CSE0001 course to develop a clear understanding of essential digital skills required in today’s world. The project includes five tasks covering digital awareness, creation of an infographic, building a professional digital portfolio, exploring coding and collaboration platforms, drafting professional emails, and understanding cybercrime and online safety. Through these tasks, I gained practical experience in using platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, HackerRank, and Google Forms. The project also helped me improve my communication skills and learn the importance of maintaining a responsible and professional online presence. Overall, this project enhanced my digital knowledge and prepared me to use technology effectively and safely in both academic and professional environments.
